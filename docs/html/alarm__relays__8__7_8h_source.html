1/*
2 * src/system/alarm_relays_8_7.h — Alarm/Slowdown/SystemFail relay
3 * interface per spec rev 07 chapter 8 (NEW in v8.6, refined in v8.7).
4 *
5 * @spec 8.7 §8 — Interface to Alarm and Monitoring System
6 *
7 * The standalone BWM system must provide three relay outputs:
8 * - Alarm relay (XS8126), normally closed: triggered at pre-warning
9 * and alarm levels. Toggled (closed-opened-closed) on level
10 * escalation. Toggle period 1 s default, adjustable 0.1–5 s.
11 * - Slowdown relay (XS8127), normally open with terminals for
12 * supervision resistor: triggered at slowdown levels.
13 * - System Fail relay (XS8129), normally closed: triggered at
14 * power fail / watchdog fail / system unavailable.
15 *
16 * In integrated systems (where the BWM is part of a larger MAS), the
17 * relays may not be physically present but the same functional
18 * triggering must be preserved.
19 *
20 * v6.20 firmware has alarm output capability via digout.c, but the
21 * XS81xx tag numbering and toggle semantics are NEW in 8.6 — Phase 4
22 * task to fully comply.
23 */
